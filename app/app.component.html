    • About this Site

    The Gainesville Permit Locator is the first part of a series of business resources being built by Code for Gainesville, the local Code for America brigade. Members of this organization donate their time and expertise in order to create tools that make government services simple, effective, and easy to use. These services are sponsored in part by the City of Gainesville.
